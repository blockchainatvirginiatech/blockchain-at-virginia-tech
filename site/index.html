What We Do

Blockchain Projects

Students come together and focus on creating a fully functional dApp. From whitepaper to production, students are involved throughout the process. All majors with different skillsets make an impact!

Crash Courses

Students learn about a variety of topics ranging from development to cryptocurrency trading. No experience is required.

Inudstry Speakers

Speakers from top blockchain companies share their experience in development, marketing and other areas. Gain valuable insights from virtual or in person meetings.

Hackathons

Collaborate with peers through blockchain focused hackathons with cryptocurrency grants and prizes. Network with industry professionals with career development opportunities.
