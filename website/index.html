Blockchain at Virginia Tech

A Blockchain Club powered by Students

About The Club

Founded by a group of students, Blockchain at Virginia Tech is a club dedicated to all things blockchain. Our club focuses on cryptocurrencies, decentralized applications and more. All students have a say in the direction of this club.

Read More

What We Do?

Students gain valuable knowledge from industry leaders, working on team projects and more. The goal is to prepare students for a career in blockchain.

Let's Start

Decentralized Applications

Students come together to work on real world blockchain projects. The goal is to produce a minimum viable product by the end of each semester. Students from different majors and experience levels are welcomed!

Cryptocurrencies

Blockchain powers cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum and Binance Coin. The value of these cryptocurrencies has surged in recent years. Open discussions related to investment opportunities is encouraged.

Education

Many students are unaware of the blockchain and the future of blockchain technology. Students learn from industry professionals through monthly events.

Weekly Meetings

Bi-weekly and monthly meetings are held to work on decentralized applications, discuss cryptocurrencies and to learn more from blockchain industry professionals.
